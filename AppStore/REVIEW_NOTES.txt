RepScroll v1.0.0 — App Review notes
====================================

HOW TO TEST (5 minutes)
-----------------------
1. Complete onboarding (5 screens). On "Pick your temptations", leave Instagram/TikTok/X enabled.
2. Home tab → tap Instagram under "Protected apps" → gate appears.
3. Tap "Earn your scroll" → allow camera → do push-ups (or hold phone and bend arms) until 10 reps.
4. Tap "Unlock Instagram" → app shows unlocked state with countdown timer.
5. Challenge tab → start a workout with front camera for squat/plank testing.
6. Settings → toggle daily reminder (optional notification permission).

SCREEN TIME / APP BLOCKING
--------------------------
v1.0 uses in-app simulation: tapping a "protected app" on Home opens RepScroll's gate flow.
Real iOS Screen Time / FamilyControls blocking is planned for v1.1.
Copy in Settings and Terms discloses this.

SUBSCRIPTIONS (StoreKit 2)
--------------------------
Product IDs (must exist in App Store Connect):
  • com.repscroll.premium.monthly — $6.99/mo, 7-day free trial
  • com.repscroll.premium.yearly  — $49.00/yr

Premium unlocks: unlimited gate challenges/day, 30-min unlock windows (vs 15 min free).
Restore purchases: Settings → Restore purchases, or Paywall → Restore purchases.

Free tier: 1 gate challenge per day.

CAMERA
------
Front camera only. On-device Vision pose detection. No recording, no upload, no network calls for video.

ACCOUNT
-------
No login required. All data local (Core Data + UserDefaults).

DEMO ACCOUNT
------------
Not applicable — no server authentication.
