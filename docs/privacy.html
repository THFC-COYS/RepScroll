RepScroll Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 29, 2026

RepScroll ("we", "the app") is built privacy-first. Workout data stays on your iPhone.

What we collect

Nothing is collected or transmitted. RepScroll does not operate backend servers for personal data. We do not sell data or use third-party analytics SDKs.

Camera & pose detection

The camera is used only for on-device Apple Vision pose detection to count reps. Video is processed in real time and is never recorded, stored, or uploaded.

Data stored on your device

  • Workout history (reps, exercise type, date) in local Core Data
  • Streak and achievement progress in UserDefaults
  • App preferences (goals, blocked apps, reminders)
  • Widget streak data in the shared App Group container

Deleting the app removes this data from your device.

Subscriptions

Premium purchases are handled entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We do not receive your payment card details.

Notifications

Daily reminders are optional and scheduled locally. No push notification servers are used.

Children

RepScroll is not directed at children under 13.

Questions: support@repscroll.app
